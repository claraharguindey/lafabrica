Everyone is aware of the standstill that has
unfortunately gripped the business life of
this industrious district. Elements that are
sarcastically said to represent the
redemption of workers have led them to such
extremes that the prosperous development
of their industries has been rendered
impossible. Those industries that were once
the pride of the Basque Region and the envy
of progress-loving peoples.
